Transport

A statement from the Secretary of State for Transport.

I envision a better Westbridge with proper traffic management, maintained transport and way more. I want to reform transportation in many ways from public transport to our roads, with my past experience at the Department of Transport I hope to reimagine Westbridge's transport system entirely whilst keeping it simple for all to understand, keeping it far from it being complex.

My end goal at the end of the day is to improve Westbridge overall to make this a way better place for all to visit and see for themselves. I will work for not for this party but for everyone.

Defence

A statement from the Secretary of State for Defence.

I hope to ensure that any Ministry of Defence protected sites are filled with members of the British Army Forces, to reduce any unknown attacks, and higher security measures. I also would like to ensure that the Command in the BAF are organised and coordinated for any planned attacks, and extend and add more divisions/teams to the BAF by returning multiple disbanded teams. Another reason is to hopefully improve the BAF and its general quality.

In addition to this, I am aiming to conduct more operations on protecting any MOD protected sites, ensuring that any whitelisted members of the BAF are trained and qualified for any unexpected attacks. I will increase the amount of active personnel if a planned attack shall be hitting on any of our protected sites.

Adding on, I will work with the Home Department and the officials to improve the safety of the areas in Westbridge. I hope to work with other ministers of state to ensure police are dispatched to patrol in various areas, to reduce any organised crime, including intelligence companies.

My ultimate goal is to ensure that all BAF sites are protected from various attacks, and ensure BAF personnel are active in Likely to be attacked areas.

Yours Truly,

zacksmendad
Secretary of State for Defence

Policing

A statement from the Minister of State for Policing, Crime Prevention & Border Affairs.

I want to crack down heavily on the various organised crime gangs operating directly out of Westbridge and County Lines, which includes working very closely with the Metropolitan Police and other forces to ensure that more enabling policies and legislation are introduced to broaden their range of powers to enable themselves to take down these barbaric criminals and put an end to organised crime.

I also aim to increase police presence in highly populated communal areas within the borough by about 32%, with the majority being unarmed officers to avoid public speculation and fear, and to give a friendlier and personal, less tactical approach to community and neighbourhood policing.

I aim to additionally crack down hard on illegal immigration within the borough, the main priority being the amendment of policies within the Immigration Act which enable immigration officers to carry firearms and personal safety equipment once again, and to improve the way we track down and detain those residing illegally in the United Kingdom.

My ultimate goal is to ensure that the people of this country are protected, and that they do not have to live in constant fear of violence, intimidation and an anarchic society full of rebellious criminals, whilst also ensuring that policing is approached properly, and to the full blast of the capabilities of all forces.

Yours Truly,

n00b3zn
Minister of State for Policing, Crime Prevention & Border Affairs
